About Los Angeles Sun Times

Los Angeles enjoys year-round pleasant sunrise and sunset times due to its Mediterranean climate. The city's west-facing beaches offer spectacular Pacific Ocean sunset views.

Best Times for Photography

Golden Hour: The hour before sunset (12:47 AM - 1:47 AM) provides the warmest, most flattering light for photography. This is when the light has a golden quality that's perfect for portraits and landscapes.

Blue Hour: 20-30 minutes after sunset when the sky turns deep blue but artificial lights are visible. Perfect for cityscape photography with balanced exposure.

Seasonal Variations

Los Angeles sun times vary throughout the year. Summer brings earlier sunrises and later sunsets, while winter has later sunrises and earlier sunsets. The exact times depend on your latitude and local terrain.
